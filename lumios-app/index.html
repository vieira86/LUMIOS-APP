Introducing LUMIOS: Revolutionizing Spectral Analysis and Beyond!/h2>

In the quest to optimize the exploration of matrices derived from cocoa fermentation, we proudly present the LUMIOS app. This groundbreaking multitask tool processes mass spectral data autonomously, streamlining the desreplication process. But that's not all! LUMIOS harnesses the power of artificial intelligence to detect molecules with pharmacological potential targeting respiratory diseases, including Covid-19 and asthma.

Key features of LUMIOS include:
  • Pattern Recognition: Pinpoint specific molecular patterns associated with respiratory ailments.
  • Autonomous Desreplication: Say goodbye to manual interventions and trust LUMIOS to efficiently sort your data.
  • Molecular Docking Core: An accurate and reliable approach for modeling and simulating molecules. LUMIOS stands out with its unique capability to test molecular annotations against four distinct targets linked to respiratory tract diseases.
  • Quick Analysis: Time is of the essence. LUMIOS swiftly analyzes your data, delivering invaluable insights when it matters most.
Harness the future of spectral analysis with LUMIOS today!
